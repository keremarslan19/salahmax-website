How it works

Three honest steps. No onboarding theater.

I

Download, set your location.

SalahMax pulls prayer times for your exact city. Choose your madhab, your calculation method, and step into the app.

→
II

Track every prayer you make.

Tap as you pray. The app remembers. Fajr alarm wakes you the first morning.

→
III

Watch your imaan compound.

Day by day, the chart becomes a year. The year becomes a habit. The habit becomes a self that prays.

→
