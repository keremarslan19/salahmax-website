Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Introduction

SalahMax ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials through Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In.
  • Prayer data. Your prayer tracking data, streaks, and progress are stored to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Location data. With your permission, we access your location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is processed on-device and not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data. We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience.

How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the prayer tracking service
  • To calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
  • To send prayer reminders and notifications (with your consent)
  • To enable community features such as groups and leaderboards
  • To process subscription payments through Apple's App Store
  • To improve our app and user experience

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google). Authentication, database, and cloud messaging.
  • RevenueCat. Subscription management.
  • Apple StoreKit. In-app purchase processing.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Your rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Withdraw location permissions at any time

Children's privacy

SalahMax is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new version within the app.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach out at support@salahmax.com.
